The problem

In Vancouver’s climate, moss is not cosmetic.

Moss spreads quickly, grows into roof surfaces, traps moisture and can shorten roof life. Algae creates dark staining, while lichens can remove protective layers from shingles and tiles.

☔

Moisture gets trapped

Moss holds water against the roof in wet coastal weather.

⚠️

Damage accelerates

Roots can lift, loosen and deteriorate roofing materials.

🏠

Curb appeal drops

Dark streaks and moss make a good home look neglected.
